What is One Rep Max?

Your One Rep Max (1RM) is the maximum amount of weight you can lift for one full repetition of an exercise. Knowing your 1RM helps you build strength programs, track progress, and avoid injury.

What is RPE?

RPE stands for Rate of Perceived Exertion. It's a scale from 6 to 10 that describes how hard a set feels. For example, RPE 10 means absolute max effort — you couldn't do another rep. This calculator uses your RPE and reps to estimate your 1RM accurately.
